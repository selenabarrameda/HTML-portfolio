The Best Webtoon Series According to Selena !

My top 3 series of all time:

The Odd Girl Out

This is my favorite because I like Nari's (the protagonist of the story) character development. I also enjoyed seeing the journey that made her become the person who she is. Not to mention the power of their friendship !

Seasons of Blossom

I very like the art style as well as the story ! The dialogues are just too perfect I can't handle it ! The emotions were properly coveyed which made me feel and enjoy reading every episode of it !

Maybe Meant To Be

The main characters' relationship is just so cute and innocent that my heart flutters too. Even the side couple in the webtoon has a cute chemistry with each other !
